Title: Introduction to LSTM Networks

LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) is a type of recurrent neural network architecture. 
It was designed to solve the vanishing gradient problem in traditional RNNs.

The forget gate in LSTM controls what information to discard from the cell state.
The formula is: ft = sigmoid(Wf * [h(t-1), xt] + bf)

LSTMs are used in sequence prediction, NLP tasks, and time series forecasting.
The key advantage is the ability to learn long-term dependencies in sequential data.
